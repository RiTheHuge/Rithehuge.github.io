About Me

I am currently pursuing a degree in Mathematical Sciences at the University of Bath. My academic journey has fostered a strong interest in data science, where I find the intersection of mathematics and coding truly fascinating. Proficient in several programming languages, including R and Python, I am equipped with the technical skills necessary to effectively analyze and extract insights from data.

Modules in my degree include:

  1. Introduction to data science
  2. Modelling and dynamical systems
  3. Functional programming
  4. Ordinary Differential Equations & Control Theory
  5. Algebra
  6. Analysis

At the University of Bath, I am a Gold Scholar; as a gold Scholar, I must complete 50 hours of volunteering, fundraising, or outreach a year and attend personal development, networking, and skills training sessions throughout each year I am at the university.


At university I have also been able to take part in ReadyTraderGo, a student coding competition that dives into the world of algorithmic trading, working in a group of three, my role being data analyst, and Leadership 123, a course offered by Coaching Focus to enhance my leadership skills and ready me for the cooperate world

Volunteer with VCode

I have actively contributed to my local community by organizing and facilitating coding workshops specifically designed for female secondary school students.
